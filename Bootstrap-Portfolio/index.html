About Me

Hello world! My name is Crystal, and I am brand new to the wonderful world of programming. I began my professional career at the ripe age of 18 when I landed a job as an intern at a medical billing company. Fast forward 11 years and I am still in the medical billing industry, but got quite the taste of technology along the way. The healthcare industry has a larger technology component than the average person may expect. From the initial encounter at the hospital or doctor's office, to the payment made by the insurance companies, there is an imense amount of information that needs to be tracked, organized, and exchanged electronically.

Many companies offer proprietary software as a service so that hospitals, doctors offices, and other facilities can quickly and efficiently bill for services. Working at one of these companies is where I got a small glimpse into the programming required to create such impressive and complex software. This exposure, combined with my lifelong appreciation of logic and structure, led to my enrollment in the CWRU Bootcamp. I'm excited to see where this new chapter in my career will take me!
